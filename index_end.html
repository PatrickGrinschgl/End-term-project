Pedestrian Comfort and Stress in Graz – Innere Stadt

We want to explore how people experience walking in the first district of Graz (Innere Stadt). We aim to compare places that feel comfortable and pleasant for walking with places that feel stressful or uncomfortable.

How to participate:

  • Click on the map to mark a place where walking feels comfortable or stressful Then select the experience type and a reason. You can add as many markers as you like, just click on the button "Save Location" after every entry.

Participation is voluntary and anonymous. No personal identifying data is collected.
