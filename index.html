Pedestrian Comfort and Stress in Graz – Innere Stadt

We want to explore how people experience walking in the first district of Graz (Innere Stadt). We aim to compare places that feel comfortable for walking with places that feel stressful.

How to participate:

  • Move the map so that the location you want to mark is centered under the red crosshair (+) inside the district boundary. Then select the experience type (comfortable, stressed, socialize, alone), choose a reason, and leave a short comment for clarification. You can add as many markers as you like. Click "Save Location" after each entry. When finished, click "Download GeoJSON".

Participation is voluntary and anonymous. No personal identifying data is collected.
